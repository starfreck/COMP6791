Students with children

Trying to study while raising a family? Feeling isolated? Connect with the Concordia University Student Parents Centre (CUSP)! We provide an accessible space to study but also a place to share interests and develop a support network. Join our community and we can help you achieve your academic goals.

BECOME A MEMBER

VOLUNTEER WITH US

Our Events

We offer a variety of weekly events, for both students and their children, from conversation groups in French and English, Storytime, Art Classes, Homework Cub, and many more!

Registration is required to all our events.

Affordable food

In collaboration with both our Professionals in Student Services Departments and external Professionals, CUSP offers a wide range of workshops tailored to your specific needs as student parents.  The workshops cover topics such as managing your finances, creating and maintaining balance in your life, all to help you succeed in your academic career.
