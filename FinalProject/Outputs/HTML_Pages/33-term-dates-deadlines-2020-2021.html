** a $80 late registration fee will be charged for registration of Fall (2202) or Fall/Winter (2203) courses made beginning September 8 or Winter (2204) courses made beginning January 13 if not already registered for any Fall (2202) or Winter (2204) courses.

*For a listing of fall and winter courses held outside the standard term dates, please see the non-standard dates and deadlines below.

Important:

  • Due to increase of activity on the system and the speed of your internet connection, we recommend you do not wait until the last hour to withdraw from your course(s). All transactions must be completed and confirmed before 12 a.m. in order to meet the deadlines.

  • The DNE deadline date (dropping a course without having to pay for it) for Fall (2202) and Fall/Winter (2203) courses is September 21, 2020. For Winter (2204) courses the DNE deadline date is January 26, 2021. Students who drop their courses after the DNE date will receive a DISC notation on their record and be responsible for full payment of the tuition and other associated fees.
